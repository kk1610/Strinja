About us

I am thrilled to welcome you to the Strinja family, a venture that has been a dream for a while. As a founder, I want to share why we embarked on this journey. Each string of art creates thousands of memories, these arts are generally made by unemployed, uneducated women in rural and urban areas with the help of some engineers.
Each customer is helping in women empowerment. With every purchase, you become a crucial part of this narrative, contributing to the empowerment of these talented women and helping us weave a tapestry of positive change.
At Strinja, we believe that art goes beyond aesthetics; it's a powerful tool for social transformation and economic empowerment.

Mission Statement

  1. Prioritize employment for the uneducated, especially women in rural and urban areas.
  2. Advocate for handmade arts, influencing a global appreciation for craftsmanship.
  3. Ensure global accessibility all over world at best price for our handcrafted products.
  4. Embrace sustainable practices, minimizing our environmental impact.
  5. Foster community engagement through workshops, events, and collaborative initiatives.

Vision

To perpetuate the essence of traditional handmade arts, Strinja envisions a sustainable, eco-friendly environment.
We aspire to create a harmonious blend of cultural preservation, aesthetic excellence, and responsible practices, ensuring that our handcrafted creations not only endure through time but also contribute positively to the world we inhabit.
